Introduction to the Dataset

This dashboard provides an interactive exploration of a heart disease dataset, helping to visualize key health indicators and their relationship with the presence of heart disease. The dataset includes various medical attributes such as age, sex, cholesterol levels, maximum heart rate, and different types of chest pain.

Use the interactive charts below to uncover patterns and insights that can aid in understanding the factors contributing to heart disease.
